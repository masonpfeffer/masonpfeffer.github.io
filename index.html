Mason Pfeffer

Learn a little bit more about Mason Pfeffer and his involvement at LSU!

Mason is a Junior Information Systems & Decision Sciences major at Louisiana State University. On campus, Mason is involved in the LSU Student Government as the Assistant Director of Finance and a voting member of the Programming Support & Initiative Funding (PSIF) commitee. He also serves as the Co-Chair of Marketing for the Association of Information Technology Professionals (AITP) and is the President of Hillel at LSU. Additionally, Mason is a member of the Delta Sigma Pi business fraternity where he has previously served as the Brotherhood Chair.
